# Welcome to the Evolamus Computer Labs

This website contains the lab assignments for the Evolution of Language & Music course. You can access the assignments through the menu on the left.

All assignments can be opened using Google Colab, by clicking the "Open in Colab" button at the top of the page. Once on Colab, **don't forget to make a copy of the notebook in your own Drive to save your changes**. If you prefer working in your own local jupyter environment, you can also download the .ipynb file.

For the _Bioacoustics in Praat_ lab, you will need to download the [Praat](https://www.fon.hum.uva.nl/praat/) software.
